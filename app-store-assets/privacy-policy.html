Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 4, 2025

The Blacklist - FBI Database

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

The Blacklist - FBI Database app operates entirely offline on your device. No data is collected, stored on external servers, or shared with third parties.

Data Storage

All app data (criminal profiles, case files, and any user-created content) is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Internet Connection

This app does not require an internet connection and does not communicate with any external servers.

Children's Privacy

This app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to Privacy Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be updated in the app and on this page.

Contact Information
